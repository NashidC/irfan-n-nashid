For the love of my life

Today is very special because it's the day I was able to call you mine! Meeting you has been one of the best things that's happened to me. How lucky am I to have someone as loving, caring, and sweet as you. From beginning to end of this year you have shown me that this love was well worth waiting for. Your sweet face is enough to cheer up the worst of my days and I never have to feel alone because I know you'll always be there. I cannot imagine my life without you, and you make me strive to be better for the both of us. My heart and my soul belongs to you and you only. And when I'm with you, nothing else matters in this world.
Happy Anniversary Shuna, 1 year down, ∞ to go!

Poems

Things I wrote for you!

February 14th, 2025

I'm glad that you're mine because you're irresistible in every way
Rightfully lucky to hear your voice everyday
Finding you was a treasure in the rough
And the way you show me sweetness is more than enough
Now I really like you and I hope to see many more valentines where you're with me

July 1st, 2025

Morning breath kisses, wake me up in delight
Forehead kisses make sure I sleep well at night
I listen to your breathing, at first it's very soft and sweet
But then picks up like thunder, that's when I know you're asleep
I don't mind the sound, it's soothing like the rain
I know that's how I love you, you're never a pain
I curl up on your chest, my favorite place to be
And I just can't wait until you spend everyday with me

September 9th, 2025

Miles away, too far from me
At least the moon is bright for both of us to see
I wish you were here
Just grabbing my waist
Maybe we could dance under the moonlight with no haste
The days are blurring because I miss you all the time
You're my love and my soul
I wish we could settle down and unwind
I know we have forever to go and I'm not in a rush
But boy do I wish we could be together always so I wouldn't have to miss you as much

September 10th, 2025

I am but a broken dish, pieces scattered everywhere
Thrown around, haven't been held gently as I wished, no one seemed to care
You pick up the pieces and try to make me whole
It's okay when I break down, you start again and this time weaving the cracks with gold
Mended carefully, you make sure to be gentle and kind
Piece by piece you make sure that you're taking your time
You know you have a lifetime to show care for me
It's okay if I break, you would do it again, happily
I'm so glad I have someone i can finally trust
You are my peace and I just love you so much

November 10th, 2025

It's night of Halloween and we decide on what to do
We land on skulls, which is fun but so much makeup to get through
I carefully carve bones into my face
You look at me every now and then, I like when we're at my place
Then it's your turn, I have to draw bones on you
And this is how I know that our love is real and true
You look in my eyes, first left and then right
Then a soft smile comes out, but ever so bright
It's in this moment that I thought, I could do this for a while
Together until we're bones, you're my reason to smile

December 10th, 2025

I think I'm in love, is that safe to say?
That i don't have to worry for the rest of my days
I try to explain this feeling because I haven't dealt with it before
It's really confusing for me when you care, you mean say you think of me as all yours?
We plan a great future, one with kids and a place
I hope we have a warm big house, I'll cook all the food, to your specific taste
Don't worry about laundry, I'll make sure to wash our clothes
You're my golden snitch, I'll open at the close
I guess that's what I have to remember, the fact that you're always there
For me when I'm happy, sad or angry, doesn't matter where
Something about looking forward, I'm happy it's with you
I'm counting down the minutes until we say I do
